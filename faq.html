Platform & Programs
What is CoachAndTrack? ▼
CoachAndTrack is a fitness coaching marketplace that connects clients with certified personal trainers and nutrition coaches. We're currently in early access — you can try sample workouts and nutrition plans for free right now, and join the waitlist for full platform access when we launch.
What programs are available for free? ▼
During early access, you can try Strength Foundation — Day 1 (an 11-movement full-body strength workout) and Nutrition Plan — Week 1 (a 7-day gluten-free bilingual meal plan). Both are fully functional with video guidance and progress tracking.
Do I need any equipment to follow the workouts? ▼
Our Strength Foundation program uses minimal equipment — a resistance band, a set of dumbbells, and a mat are all you need. Modifications are available for most movements. The nutrition plan requires no equipment at all.
Are programs available in Spanish? ▼
Yes! Our nutrition plans include a full Spanish translation — just toggle EN / ES at the top of the page. Both Veronica Mosquera and Rolando Corzo are bilingual, and their coaching content is available in English and Spanish. We welcome coaches of all language backgrounds on the platform.
Coaches & Pricing
How are coaches verified? ▼
Every coach on CoachAndTrack goes through a three-step verification: we check their certifications (ISSA, NASM, ACE, or equivalent), review their coach profile for accuracy, and approve all program content before it goes live. You'll see a "Verified Coach" badge on any profile that has passed this process.
How much does CoachAndTrack cost? ▼
Sample programs are free during early access. At launch, we plan to offer a Pro plan and an Elite plan — founding members who join the waitlist will receive special early-bird pricing. Full pricing details will be shared with waitlist members before launch.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. CoachAndTrack subscriptions are cancel-anytime — no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll retain access through the end of your billing period. We believe in earning your subscription every month.
I'm a coach. How do I apply to join the platform? ▼
Visit our Become a Coach page to learn about requirements, earnings potential, and the application process. Coaches keep 70% of all subscription revenue. We're selectively onboarding certified coaches who are passionate about delivering results.
