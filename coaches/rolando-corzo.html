His Story

ABOUT ROLANDO

Rolando Corzo didn't stumble into fitness — he was built for it. Over the past decade he has worked with everyone from beginners taking their first steps in the gym to competitive bodybuilders and high-performance athletes. His approach has been shaped by thousands of hours on the floor, coaching real people through real challenges.

What sets Rolando apart is the depth of his expertise. Five ISSA certifications — Personal Trainer, Corrective Exercise Specialist, Specialist in Strength and Conditioning, Glute Specialist, and Nutritionist — mean he can address the full picture: movement quality, injury prevention, progressive strength, body composition, and diet. He doesn't just train bodies — he fixes them and builds them back up right.

"Ten years in, I still get fired up every time a client hits a new personal best. That's why I do this. Your goal is my goal — and I don't stop until we get there."
— Rolando Corzo

Based in Los Angeles and coaching in both English and Spanish, Rolando makes high-level fitness guidance accessible to a wider community. He believes your language, your background, and your starting point should never limit your access to great coaching.

CoachAndTrack is where he's expanding his reach — bringing the same intensity and precision that his in-person clients know to an online platform built for results.
