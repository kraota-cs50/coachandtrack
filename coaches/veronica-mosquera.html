Her Story

ABOUT VERONICA

Veronica M. has been in the fitness industry for five years — not as a trend, but as a calling. After seeing firsthand how confusing and inaccessible fitness content can be, especially for Spanish-speaking communities, she became determined to change that. She pursued dual certifications in Personal Training and Nutrition through ISSA and hasn't looked back.

Her coaching style is direct, supportive, and grounded in evidence. She doesn't believe in extreme diets, impossible workout schedules, or one-size-fits-all plans. Everything she designs is built around the individual — their schedule, their goals, their body, and their life.

"Fitness should make your life better, not harder. My job is to meet you where you are and help you get to where you want to be — in English or Spanish, whatever works for you."
— Veronica M.

Based in Los Angeles, Veronica works with clients across a range of goals — from building their first strength foundation to navigating fat loss and muscle recomposition after years of yo-yo dieting. Her bilingual approach means no one gets left behind due to a language barrier.

CoachAndTrack is where she's building her online presence to reach more people than in-person sessions ever could.
