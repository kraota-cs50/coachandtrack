Her Story

ABOUT ELIANA

Eliana Soto believes that a strong core is the foundation of everything — not just in fitness, but in life. Her coaching philosophy is built around helping people develop real functional strength that transfers beyond the gym, combined with nutrition habits that fuel sustainable progress.

With a specialization in core training and a deep understanding of nutrition science, Eliana designs programs that address the whole picture — movement quality, strength, and fueling your body right. Her bilingual coaching means she can connect authentically with both English and Spanish speaking clients.

"A strong core isn't just about abs — it's about stability, power, and confidence in everything you do. When your foundation is solid, everything else follows."
— Eliana Soto

Eliana works with clients at all levels — from beginners building their first real training foundation to athletes looking to improve performance through better core stability and nutrition. Her approach is direct, encouraging, and always grounded in what actually works.

CoachAndTrack is where she's bringing her expertise online to reach more people who are ready to build something real.
