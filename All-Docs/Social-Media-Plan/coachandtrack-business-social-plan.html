Section 02

Two Distinct Audiences

CoachAndTrack needs to speak to two very different people. Each requires tailored messaging, platforms, and content types.

Audience A
People Seeking a Coach & Progress Tracking
Fitness-motivated individuals who want a certified, matched personal trainer and the tools to track, visualise, and share their progress journey. They're motivated by results, accountability, and community.
Best platforms: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook Group
  • "Find your perfect coach — matched to your goals"
  • "Track every rep, every run, every result"
  • "Share your progress. Stay accountable."
  • "Your coach is one tap away"
  • "Real coaches. Real plans. Real results."
Audience B
Fitness Coaches & Personal Trainers
Certified trainers and coaches who want more clients, a professional platform to manage them, and tools to scale their coaching business online. They care about credibility, income, and ease of use.
Best platforms: LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook Groups (fitness pro communities)
  • "Get matched with clients who are serious about results"
  • "Manage all your clients in one platform"
  • "We handle the discovery. You focus on coaching."
  • "Join our founding coach cohort — limited spots"
  • "Grow your coaching business online with CoachAndTrack"
💡 Content Split Guideline Aim for roughly 70% client-focused content (discovery, fitness tips, progress tracking) and 30% coach-focused content (coach spotlights, "join our platform" calls to action, professional benefits). This reflects your primary monetisation path while building the coach supply side in parallel.

Section 05

Waitlist Strategy

Every social post should feed into one goal: getting people onto your waitlist. Here's how to build it systematically.

1
🔗
Set Up a Waitlist Page
Create a simple landing page (Mailchimp, Tally, or Carrd) with an email capture. This is the single destination for all your "link in bio" traffic. Keep it to one screen: headline, 3 bullet benefits, email field, submit button.
2
📌
Pin It Everywhere
Instagram bio link, TikTok bio link, Facebook Page CTA button, LinkedIn Company Page website field. Every profile should point to one URL. Use a link-in-bio tool (like Linktree or Bio.link) to track clicks per platform.
3
🎁
Add an Incentive
Give people a reason to join now, not later. Ideas: "First 100 get free premium for 3 months", "Founding members get exclusive coach matching priority", or "Early access before public launch." Scarcity drives action.
4
📣
Share the Number
Post updates like "127 people have joined the waitlist — are you in?" Social proof creates FOMO and drives more sign-ups. Share waitlist milestones (100, 250, 500) as celebration posts to generate engagement.
📧 What to Send Waitlist Members

Once someone joins your waitlist, send them 3 emails over 14 days: (1) Welcome + what to expect, (2) a value email with a free fitness resource (this builds goodwill and reduces unsubscribes), and (3) a launch-day email with their exclusive early access link. Keep it personal and founder-voiced — not corporate.

Section 06

Attracting Coaches to the Platform

Without coaches, there's no marketplace. Here's how to use social media to recruit founding coaches in the next 14 days.

🎯 LinkedIn Outreach Posts

Post a "We're looking for founding coaches" article on LinkedIn 2× per week. Be specific: type of coaches you want, what they get (early access, profile visibility, revenue share details). Personal founder posts outperform company page posts — use both.

📣 Facebook Group Seeding

Join 5–10 personal trainer and fitness coach Facebook Groups. Share value content (not spam) for 3–4 days before mentioning CoachAndTrack. Look for groups like "Online Personal Trainers", "Fitness Coaches UK/US", "Personal Trainer Business Tips".

🎬 Coach-Specific TikTok/Reels

Create a short series: "If you're a fitness coach, watch this." Address their pain points (finding clients, managing bookings, building online presence) and position CoachAndTrack as the solution. Use hashtags like #personaltrainertips #fitnesscoach.

🤝 DM Outreach Campaign

Identify 20–30 coaches on Instagram with 1,000–10,000 followers (micro-influencers). Send personalised DMs: acknowledge their work, explain the platform, offer founding coach benefits. Aim for quality over quantity — 5 genuine replies beats 50 cold blasts.

🎵 TikTok: Keywords beat hashtags On TikTok, use only 3–5 hashtags per video. The algorithm is keyword-based — write descriptive captions that include natural language like "how to find a personal trainer" or "fitness progress tracking app". Always include #FitnessApp and one trending hashtag from the Discover page. Your caption words drive reach more than hashtags do.
