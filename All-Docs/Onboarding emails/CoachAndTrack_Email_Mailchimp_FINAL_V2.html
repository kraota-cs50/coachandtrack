Welcome to CoachAndTrack — You're a Founding Coach

Hi Coach,

We're thrilled to have you as one of the very first coaches on CoachAndTrack — the marketplace connecting clients with certified fitness professionals.

As a Founding Coach, you'll work directly with us at every step — personal attention, direct communication, and a real role in shaping the platform from the inside.

ONBOARDING PROCESS

How it works right now

The platform is in its launch phase, so the process is personal and hands-on. Here's what we'll do together:

1

Send us your profile information

Reply to this email with: a professional photo, a short bio (2–3 sentences), your certifications, and specialties. We'll build your profile on the platform for you.
2

Sign the Trainer Agreement

Review the attached document, sign it, and return a scanned copy to support@coachandtrack.com.
3

Set up your Stripe account

You'll need to create your own free account at stripe.com. We'll send you separate step-by-step instructions to connect it with CoachAndTrack so your 70% earnings are deposited directly to you.
4

Record your starter program

Record a 2-week program (8–10 videos). When ready, upload them to Google Drive or WeTransfer and send us the link. We handle publishing them to the platform.
5

Your profile goes live!

Once everything is set, we'll activate your profile on coachandtrack.com and send you your personal link to share with your clients and community.

EARNINGS

Your cut: 70% revenue share

You earn 70% of every client subscription.

Clients subscribe at Pro ($14/mo) or Elite ($29/mo). Payments go directly to your Stripe account. CoachAndTrack retains 30% as a platform fee.

VIDEO TIPS

You don't need a professional studio

📱 Camera

Your smartphone records excellent 1080p. Shoot horizontal. Use a tripod or stacked books for stability.

💡 Lighting

Face a window for natural light. A $30–50 ring light is a great upgrade. Never shoot with light behind you.

🎙️ Audio

A clip-on lavalier mic ($20–40) makes a huge difference. Record in a quiet room, avoid echo.

📐 Framing

Full body visible. Phone ~6–8 ft away. Keep intro under 15 seconds — get right to the workout.

CONTENT STRUCTURE

How to structure your workouts

Phase 1 — 2-week starter program: Record 8–10 complete videos before we publish your profile. This ensures new clients find content immediately.

Phase 2 — Weekly cadence: After launch, add 2–4 new videos per week on a consistent schedule.

Each session: Warm-up → Main workout → Cool-down. Include difficulty level and duration in the title.

PROCESO DE INCORPORACIÓN

Cómo funciona ahora mismo

La plataforma está en su fase de lanzamiento, así que el proceso es personal y directo. Aquí está lo que haremos juntos:

1

Envíanos tu información de perfil

Responde a este correo con: foto profesional, biografía corta (2–3 oraciones), certificaciones y especialidades. Nosotros construiremos tu perfil en la plataforma por ti.

Firma el Acuerdo de Entrenador

Revisa el documento adjunto, fírmalo y devuelve una copia escaneada a support@coachandtrack.com.

Graba tu programa de inicio

INGRESOS

Los clientes se suscriben al plan Pro ($14/mes) o Elite ($29/mes). Los pagos llegan directamente a tu cuenta de Stripe. CoachAndTrack retiene el 30% como tarifa de plataforma.

CONSEJOS DE GRABACIÓN

No necesitas un estudio profesional

📱 Cámara

Tu smartphone graba en 1080p excelente. Graba horizontal. Trípode o libros apilados para estabilidad.

💡 Iluminación

Colócate frente a una ventana. Un aro de luz ($30–50) es una gran mejora. Nunca con luz detrás de ti.

🎙️ Audio

Micrófono de solapa ($20–40). Graba en silencio, evita habitaciones con eco.

📐 Encuadre

Cuerpo completo visible. Teléfono a ~2 metros. Intro bajo 15 segundos, luego directo al entrenamiento.

ESTRUCTURA DE CONTENIDO

Cómo estructurar tus entrenamientos

Fase 1 — Programa de 2 semanas: Graba 8–10 videos completos antes de que publiquemos tu perfil. Esto garantiza que los nuevos clientes encuentren contenido inmediatamente.

Fase 2 — Cadencia semanal: Después del lanzamiento, agrega 2–4 videos nuevos por semana de forma consistente.

Cada sesión: Calentamiento → Entrenamiento principal → Enfriamiento. Incluye nivel de dificultad y duración en el título.
