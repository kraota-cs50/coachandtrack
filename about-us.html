How We Got Here

The Story Behind CoachAndTrack

CoachAndTrack started with a frustration. Finding a good fitness coach online was harder than it should be. Platforms were cluttered, pricing was opaque, and there was no good way to track progress between sessions.

"What if there was one place where great coaches and motivated people could find each other — and actually track the results together?"

That question became CoachAndTrack. We built a platform where certified coaches can reach more clients, and where clients can find the right coach for their specific goal — whether that's weight loss, strength, flexibility, nutrition, or simply building a healthier lifestyle.

We are pre-launch and onboarding our founding coaches right now. This is the ground floor. The coaches who join today will shape the platform, and the members who sign up now will lock in founding member pricing before we open to the public.

We are building CoachAndTrack in public — one coach, one member, one workout at a time. We can't wait for you to be part of it.
